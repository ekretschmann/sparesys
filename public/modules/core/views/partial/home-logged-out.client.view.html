Rememberators optimize your learning
Spaced repetition tools are great, but they expect you to do your chores every day. Remembarators does not have this unrealistic ecpectation and adapts to you and your learning style, whether you repeat your cards every day, or you are just coming back from a break, or you decide to refresh your knowledge after years.
Public courses - Private copies
You create your own stacks of cards just for yourself. If you feel like it, you can make them publically accessible. When you enroll in a public course, you work on your local copy, where you can add cards or edit them as you wish.
Rememberators learn about your learning
Some things are easier to remember than others. Why waste time repeating things often that are easy to remember anyway? Rememberators keep track on what people remember easily and asks these cards less often.
Rememberators let you set priorities
Some things are more important to remember than others. You can tell the Rememberators what is essential for you to know and they will make sure that you won't forget those.
You schedule your learning
You have to know your stack at a certain date? Tell the Rememberators, and they will optimize your learning so that you succeed.
