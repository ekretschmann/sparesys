Use this tab to change card settings. The language settings are used for the reading out loud and speech recognition features. You can also select the checking method, the due and start date for the card. With the in the corner, you can delete this card. and will navigate to the previous and next card of the pack. Forward Mode settings can be changed here. If you want to use the forward mode, you can use text to speech to read questions or answers to the student. When you select speech recognition, you will be able to use your microphone to say your answer. Don't forget to configure the language your cards are in on the Cards tab! Reverse Mode settings can be changed here. If you want to use the forward mode, you can use text to speech to read out questions or answers. When you select speech recognition, you will be able to use your microphone to speak the answer. Don't forget to configure what language your cards are in on the Cards tab! Image Mode settings can be changed here. The Manage Images link will let you search for images that you can use instead of text on the card front. You can also show the text and read it out loud. You can make use of the speech recognition feature as input and read the answers back to the student.
