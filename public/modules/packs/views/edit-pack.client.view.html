For packs, there is only one setting to change, which is the name of the pack. With the in the corner, you can delete this pack. and will navigate to the previous and next pack of the course. From here you can browse to the cards in your pack by clicking on the card names. You can also add new cards by clicking on the icon. Rememberators will introduce new cards in the order they find them in the packs. You can rearrange the card order by dragging and dropping the most important packs to the top of the list. You can change the settings of all your cards on this tab. You can configure the languages used for reading out loud and speech recognition. You can choose how your answers are going to be checked and also configure a start date from when on the card will be practiced and a due date at which you want to be sure you remember the card. Forward Mode settings can be changed here. If you want to use the forward mode, you can use text to speech to read questions or answers to the student. When you select speech recognition, you will be able to use your microphone to say your answer. Don't forget to configure the language your cards are in on the Cards tab!
