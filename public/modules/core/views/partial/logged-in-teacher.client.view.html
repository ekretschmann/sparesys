Teaching
If you are a teacher and your school is set up with Rememberators, use the settings in the {{user.displayName}} menu to register for the role of a teacher. You will get a Teaching menu with all the details to manage your students. Rememberators organise teaching in schools and classes. If you just want to coach your own children, it is easy to register your own school and classes. Become a school admin and follow the instructions to set yourself up as teacher for your children.
