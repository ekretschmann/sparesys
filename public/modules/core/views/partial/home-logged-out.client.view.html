Repeaterator optimized learning
Spaced repetition tools are great, but they expect you to do your chores every day. Yes chores. Repeaterator doesn't. It adapts to you, whether you repeat your cards every day, or you are just coming back from a break, or you decide to refresh your knowledge after years.
Public courses - Private copies
You can create your own stacks of cards just for yourself. If you feel like it, you can make them publically accessible. When you enroll in a public course, you work on your local copy. You can add cards or edit them as you wish.
Sparesys learns about your learning
Some things are easier to remember than others. Why waste time repeating things often that are easy to remember anyway? For public courses, sparesys keeps track on what people remember easily and asks these cards less often.
You schedule your learning
You have to know your stack at a certain date? Tell sparesys, and it will optimize your learning so that you succeed.
