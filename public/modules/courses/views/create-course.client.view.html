Creating a new course
Wow! You are creating a course! You have to give your course a name and a short description of what your course is going to be about. Once that is done you will be able to add packs to your course and cards to the packs.
Help
Course settings
Rememberators can read questions and answer to the students of your course. If you want to use this, please define the languages to use. Self checks allow your user to rate their answers themselves. Computer checks will require typing an answer in.

Course settings
You can also configure what modes your cards should be used in by default. You can change these settings for each card individually later.
