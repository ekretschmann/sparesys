Spaced repetition tools are great, but they expect you to do your chores every day. Rememberators adapts to you and your learning style. Whether you repeat your cards every day, or are just coming back from a break. We call it maximum gain repetition.
You can create your own stacks of cards just for yourself. If you feel like it, you can make them publicly accessible. When you enroll in a public course, you work on your local copy, where you can add cards or edit them as you wish.
Some things are easier to remember than others. Why waste time repeating things often that are easy to remember anyway? Rememberators keep track of what people remember easily and asks these cards less often.
Rememberators is still in its alpha stage. We are currently including test user feedback. Rememberators is open for early adopters, but at this stage we are not advertising the application to a wide audience. But stay tuned, a beta release is planned for 2016.
