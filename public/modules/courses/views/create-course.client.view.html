Creating a Course
Wow! You are creating a course! You have to give your course a name and a short description of what your course is going to be about. Once that is done you will be able to add packs to your course and cards to the packs.

Course Properties
You can also define the languages you want to use for your cards and what modes are supported. You will be able to change these settings for the cards, but it is useful to have these in place for new cards. If you do this, you won't have to remember to set them for each and every card you create in your course.
